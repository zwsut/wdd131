Exercise Hub Site Plan - WDD131

Site Name

The name for the site, "Exercise Hub" was chosen because it will immediately be apparent to visitors what the topic of the site is. It is a brief and informative name that will not confuse visitors.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Exercise Hub is to provide users with a simple way to track and visualize workout patterns. The home page will introduce them and give them an idea of what the site is about. A second page will give them information on the type of workouts we track. On the third, users will be able to input workout information, including type, length, and date.

Scenarios

  • -What advanced features can help me analyze my workouts and improve my performance?
  • -How can I start exercising regularly and track my progress as a beginner?
  • -How can I fit effective workouts into my busy schedule and track my fitness without spending too much time?
